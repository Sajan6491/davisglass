Case study

Retail shopfront upgrade, Melbourne

Busy inner-city retailer · Laminated safety glass · Same-day turnaround

Project background

A high-traffic fashion retailer in inner-Melbourne had a cracked tempered shopfront panel after an overnight impact. The site needed to be made safe quickly, without closing the store for more than a few hours.

Solution

The glazier measured on site first thing in the morning and ordered laminated safety glass through Davis Glass. Using our stocked laminate range and cut-to-size service, the replacement panel was produced and ready for collection that afternoon.

The final specification used clear laminated safety glass with a polished edge, sized to drop straight into the existing framing. This allowed the retailer to reopen for late-afternoon trade with a compliant shopfront.

Outcome

Downtime for the store was limited to a few hours and the retailer avoided the cost and disruption of boarding the opening for several days. Using laminated glass also improved security compared with the original monolithic toughened panel.
