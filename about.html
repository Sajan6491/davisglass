WHAT WE DO

Wholesale glass supply with service to match.

Our goal is to keep your business productive — combining reliable supply with clear, friendly communication and realistic pricing.

Whether you’re cutting glass in-house or ordering units for specific jobs, our team works to keep things straightforward. We focus on accurate orders, responsive updates and reducing downtime for your crews.

We regularly supply:

  • Cut-to-size laminate, float and toughened glass.
  • Double glazed units to suit your frame systems.
  • Mirror, obscure and special products for detailed work.
  • Standard sheet sizes to help reduce cutting waste.
WHAT WE STAND FOR

Service, relationships and competitive wholesale pricing.

We look after glaziers and fabricators with fast, friendly and consistent support — without losing sight of value for money.

We know that when glass holds up a job, it creates pressure on site and with your customers. That’s why we work hard to minimise lead times, communicate clearly and keep your team moving.

Our warehouse and yard are set up so you can get in and out quickly. There’s room for vehicles, easy access for loading and staff on hand who know the product and understand trade work.

Unlike larger wholesalers, we try not to treat you like a number. Many of our customers are on a first-name basis with the team — and we value those long-term working relationships.

Whether you’re a larger operation or a mobile trade business, we’re happy to talk through pricing, options and ways to improve how we work together.

OUR STORY

From local supplier to performance glass partner.

Davis Glass has grown alongside the Australian building industry, expanding from everyday glass supply into advanced laminate and insulated systems.

We started by servicing local glaziers and builders with standard safety, mirror and float glass. As projects became more complex — and performance requirements increased — we invested in new products, knowledge and partnerships.

Today, we support everything from residential upgrades to large-scale commercial façades, helping teams navigate the mix of options now available in the market.

The goal hasn’t changed: make it easier for our customers to deliver great glass outcomes, every time.
